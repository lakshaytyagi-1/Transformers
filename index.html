Transformers Universe

Welcome to the world of robots in disguise. Here you'll meet heroes and villains locked in a battle for Earth and Cybertron alike.

Autobots

The Autobots fight to protect humanity and their home planet, Cybertron. Led by Optimus Prime, they value freedom above all else.

Decepticons

The Decepticons seek power and control. Under Megatron’s command, they aim to conquer Cybertron and beyond.

About Transformers

Transformers began as toys in the 1980s and grew into cartoons, comics, and blockbuster movies. It’s a universe of war, friendship, and giant robots morphing into cars, planes, and more.
